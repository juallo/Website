My academic journey is driven by curiosity, a love for interdisciplinary work, and the ambition to contribute to tackling the big questions: What is the brain’s code? Can we find a unified theory of brain function?

Beyond my studies, I actively engage in international and interdisciplinary projects — for example as a spokesperson of the German Academic Scholarship Foundation, as part of the organizing team of the German American Conference at Harvard, and in the leadership of the Young European Symposium.
